[QIF] Section

In this section specific details of the programs behaviour during production of QIF files can be configured.

 

DateFormat

Defines the format of dates in the QIF output. Some programs require US-format dates, others require European dates or are more flexible. Standard values are:

 

dd/mm/yy - European format, 2-digit year

dd/mm/yyyy - European format, 4-digit year

mm/dd/yy - US format, 2-digit year

mm/dd/yyyy - US format, 4-digit year

 

Default is mm/dd/yyyy.
