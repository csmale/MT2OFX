MT2OFX Main Window

The following illustration shows the main window of MT2OFX.

 

 

Click on Browse to select an MT940, CSV or other file to convert.

Click on Convert! to perform the conversion and write the result out to a file.

Click on From Clipboard to convert the contents of the clipboard.

Click on Options to open the configuration screens.

Click on Language to select the working language for the program.

Click on Close to leave the program.
